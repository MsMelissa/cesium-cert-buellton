NEW PUBLIC HEARINGS 
 
2. Resolution No. 24-04: A Resolution of the Planning Commission of the City 
of Buellton, California, Approving a Conditional Use Permit (24-CUP-02) to 
Establish a Gymnastics Facility for Pacific Flips, Located at 220 Industrial Way, 
Assessor Parcel Number 099-690-013, and Making Findings in Support Thereof 
 
RECOMMENDATION 
That the Planning Commission adopt Resolution No. 24-04 – “A Resolution of the Planning Commission of the City of Buellton, California, Approving a Conditional Use Permit (24-CUP02) to Establish a Gymnastics Facility for Pacific Flips, Located at 220 Industrial Way, Assessor Parcel Number 099-690-013, and Making Findings in Support Thereof” 
 
 STAFF REPORT 
 Assistant Planner Cara Miralles presented the staff report.   
 
 DOCUMENTS: 
 Staff report with attachments as listed in the staff report. 
 
 SPEAKERS/DISCUSSION: 
 
Commissioner Reese disclosed her father designed the shell building but she does not have a financial interest in the agenda item 
 
Commissioners asked questions regarding parking at the building, the number of parking spaces available, and parking contingency plans 
 
Kelly Reed, owner of Pacific Flips, spoke on the proposal and provided clarification on parking 
 
Commission members commented on the proposal 
 
MOTION: 
Motion by Commissioner Contreras, seconded by Vice Chair Reese approving resolution 
 
VOTE: 
Motion passed with a 4-0 voice vote. 
 
 
PLANNING DIRECTOR REPORT 
 
Planning Director Keefer announced the Cabrillo Economic Development Corporation broke ground on the Village Senior Apartments. She announced it was likely one or both meetings scheduled for September would be held. 
 
ADJOURNMENT 
 
Chair Mercado adjourned the meeting 6:24 p.m. The next regular meeting of the Planning Commission will be held on Thursday, September 5th, 2024 at 6:00 p.m.
