ELECTION OF OFFICERS 
Chair Patty Hammel announced that she is resigning from the Planning Commission effective today and recused herself from participating in the discussion and vote for items #1 and #2 on the Agenda. 
1. Election of Chair and Vice Chair 
Motion by Commissioner Reese and seconded by Commissioner DeFazio to nominate Art Mercado to serve as Planning Commission Chairman for calendar year 2024. 
Motion passed with a 4-0 roll call vote. Motion by Commissioner Sarquilla and seconded by Commissioner DeFazio to nominate Shannon Reese to serve as Planning Commission Vice Chair for calendar year 2024. 
Motion passed with a 4-0 roll call vote. The election of officers becomes effective at the next Planning Commission meeting.
