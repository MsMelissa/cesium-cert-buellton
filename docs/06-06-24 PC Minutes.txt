CITY OF BUELLTON 
PLANNING COMMISSION MEETING MINUTES 
June 6, 2024 – 6:00 p.m. City Council Chambers, 140 West Highway 246 Buellton, California 
CALL TO ORDER   
Chair Mercado called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. 
PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE  
Commissioner Contreras led the Pledge of Allegiance 
ROLL CALL  
Present:  Commissioners Daniel Contreras, Laura Romano DeFazio, Marcilo  
Sarquilla, Vice Chair Shannon Reese and Chair Art Mercado  
Staff:  Planning Director Andrea Keefer, Public Works Director Rose Hess and  
Staff Assistant/Planning Technician Clare Barcelona  

REORDERING OF AGENDA 
None 
APPROVAL OF MINUTES 
1. Minutes of the Regular Planning Commission meeting of May 2, 2024 MOTION: 
Motion by Vice Chair Reese and seconded by Commissioner Sarquilla to approve the Minutes of May 2, 2024. 
VOTE: 
Motion passed with a 4-0 voice vote with abstention by Commissioner DeFazio due to her absence from the meeting.

None

None

None

None 
CONTINUED PUBLIC HEARINGS

NEW PUBLIC HEARINGS 
2. Resolution No. 24-03 -“ A Resolution of the Planning Commission of the City of Buellton, California, Recommending City Council Adoption of an Ordinance, Amending Chapter 19.02.210 of the Buellton Municipal Code, Pertaining to Permit Requirements and Appropriate Zones for Emergency Shelters” 
SPEAKERS/DISCUSSION 
Planning Director Keefer announced that this item is continued to a date uncertain in order to allow additional time for staff to ensure consistency of the draft Ordinance with General Plan policies and procedures. 
PLANNING COMMISSIONER COMMENTS 
Commissioner Laura Romano DeFazio announced that she will be resigning from the Planning Commission effective on today’s date. 
Chair Mercado and the Commission thanked Laura for her service on the Planning Commission. 
PLANNING DIRECTOR REPORT 
Planning Director Keefer thanked Laura Romano DeFazio for her time on the Planning Commission. Planning Director Keefer and the Commission congratulated Clare Barcelona, Staff Assistant/Planning Technician on her upcoming retirement and thanked her for her 19 years of service with the City of Buellton. 
ADJOURNMENT 
Chair Mercado adjourned the meeting 6:08 p.m. The next regular meeting of the Planning Commission will be held on Thursday, June 20, 2024 at 6:00 p.m.
