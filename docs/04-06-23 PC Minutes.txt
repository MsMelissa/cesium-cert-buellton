3.        Resolution No. 23-02 - “A Resolution of the Planning Commission of the City of  Buellton, California, Approving a One-Year Time Extension (23-TE-02) Request for  Final Development Plan (16-FDP-05), Conditional Use Permit (16-CUP-02), and  Sign Ordinance Exemption (17-EXP-01) for the Commons Project, Located at  610 McMurray Road on Assessor’s Parcel Number 137-170-068, and Making  Findings in Support  Thereof”  
  
 STAFF REPORT 
 Staff Assistant/Planning Technician Clare Barcelona presented the staff report. 
 
 DOCUMENTS: 
 Staff report with attachments. 
 
 SPEAKERS/DISCUSSION 
 Mark Herthel, Applicant, addressed the Commission stating that the time extension  request is to allow time to decide if modifications to the existing entitlements can be  made and also stated that he has been approached by parties interested in purchasing the  property. 
 
 Chair Hammel opened the public hearing at 6:11 p.m.    
 There being no speaker’s Chair Hammel closed the Public Hearing at 6:12 p.m.   
 
 MOTION: 
 Vice Chair Mercado moved and Commissioner Sarquilla seconded the motion to adopt  
 Resolution No. 23-02 - “A Resolution of the Planning Commission of the City of  Buellton, California, Approving a One-Year Time Extension (23-TE-02) Request for  Final Development Plan (16-FDP-05), Conditional Use Permit (16-CUP-02), and  Sign  Ordinance Exemption (17-EXP-01) for the Commons Project, Located at  610  McMurray Road on Assessor’s Parcel Number 137-170-068, and Making  Findings in  Support Thereof” 
 
 VOTE: 
 Motion passed by a roll call vote of 5-0. 
 Vice Chair Mercado – Yes 
 Commissioner Sarquilla – Yes 
 Commissioner Romano DeFazio – Yes 
 Commissioner Reese – Yes 
 Chair Hammel - Yes
